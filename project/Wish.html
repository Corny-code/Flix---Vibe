Follows Ember and Wade, in a city where fire-, water-, earth- and air-residents live together.
A young girl named Asha, a sharp-witted idealist, who makes a wish so powerful that it is answered by a cosmic force, a little star of boundless energy called “Star” of course. Together, Asha and Star confront the ruler of Rosas, King Magnifico. King Magnifico and his wife, Queen Amaya, established the kingdom of Rosas on an island in the Mediterranean Sea to keep people safe. Having studied sorcery, Magnifico is able to grant the wishes of his subjects, who each give up the memory of their wish to be sealed and protected by the king until he decides to grant them. Once in a month, as a ceremonial event, Magnifico chooses one wish to be granted keeping all the other wishes.
